# 📡 NSE Scanner

A live Nifty 500 stock scanner that runs in your browser.

## What it does
- Scans 500 NSE stocks using Yahoo Finance data
- Detects Volume Spikes, Breakouts, VCP Patterns and more
- Sends push notifications for signals
- Auto-scans every 15 minutes during market hours (9:15 AM to 3:30 PM IST)
- Works as an installable app on Android and iPhone

## How to use
1. Open the app in your browser
2. Tap SCAN NIFTY 500 LIVE
3. Enable push notifications when asked
